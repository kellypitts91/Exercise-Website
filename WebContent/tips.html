Did you know??

For the first 15 minutes of your workout, your body burns sugars and carbs
At 30 minutes, your body enters the fat burning zone

Why work out before work??

  1. It is often the most peaceful time of the day and a great way to get some alone time.
  2. It preps you for the day, waking up your brain, clears the mind and helps organise your thoughts.
  3. Get your exercise first thing and you wont have to worry about it for the rest of the day.
  4. It boosts your metabolism and gets your digestion moving for the whole day.

10 tips to increase workout motivation

  1. Take before and after pictures
  2. Start an exercise contest
  3. Think about the benefits
  4. Make friends at the gym
  5. Load up on a new playlist
  6. Reward your efforts
  7. Take it outdoors
  8. Variety is key
  9. Find a buddy
  10. Make it fun

Tips to scheduling your workouts

  • Use a planner - Use one that breaks down each day by hours.
  • Select a time - Be specific, example: 5am, 6:30pm.
  • Stick with it! Make your workouts non negotiable!
  • Select a day to schedule your workouts for the week. example: Sundays.
  • REMEMBER to keep your goal in mind! you are worth it!

Top 5 work out tips

  2. Water Water Water!
  3. Stretch before anything!
  4. Workout partner
  5. Know what your body can and can't do!

9 everyday ways to sneak in a workout

  1. Leave a pair of dumbbells at the bottom of the stairs
  2. Practice good posture to strengthen your core
  3. 10 squats everytime you use the bathroom
  4. 50 crunches and 50 push-ups before every shower
  5. Calve raises while you wait in line
  6. Park further from your destination
  7. Bicep curl with your grocery bags
  8. Jumping jacks during TV commercial breaks
  9. Walk while you talk

Avoid Dehdration

Eat an orange before a workout, you will experience less soreness and they help you stay hydrated.

  • Strap baby to you in a baby carrier.
  • Join a gym with child-care.
  • Sign up for parent-baby yoga classes.
  • Purchase some exercise videos.
  • Improvise
  • Most importantly, put exercise on your “to-do” list.
